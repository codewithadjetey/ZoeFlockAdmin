Before Contacting Support

Please try these steps before reaching out to our support team:

Troubleshooting Steps
  1. Check Documentation: Review the relevant documentation page
  2. Search FAQ: Look for your question in our FAQ section
  3. Clear Browser Cache: Clear cookies and cache
  4. Try Different Browser: Test in Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
  5. Check Internet Connection: Ensure stable internet connection
  6. Restart Application: Log out and log back in
Quick Tips
  • Include error messages
  • Describe steps taken
  • Mention your browser
  • Provide screenshots

What to Include in Your Support Request

To help us assist you quickly and effectively, please include:

Essential Information
  • Your Name: Full name and church/organization
  • Contact Details: Email and phone number
  • Issue Description: Clear description of the problem
  • Steps Taken: What you've already tried
  • Error Messages: Exact error text if any
  • Browser Information: Browser type and version
Helpful Extras
  • Screenshots: Images of the issue
  • Video Recording: Screen recording of the problem
  • System Details: Operating system and device
  • Account Information: User role and permissions
  • Time and Date: When the issue occurred
  • Frequency: How often the issue happens
