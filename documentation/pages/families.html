Understanding Family Structure

Zoe Flock Admin allows you to organize members into family units, making it easier to:

Family Benefits
  • Better Organization: Group related members together
  • Family Head: Designate a primary contact person
  • Attendance Tracking: Track family attendance patterns
  • Communication: Send messages to entire families
  • Financial Management: Track family tithes and donations
Family Roles
  • Family Head: Primary contact and decision maker
  • Spouse: Partner of the family head
  • Children: Dependent family members
  • Extended Family: Other relatives living together
  • Dependents: Non-relatives under family care

Creating Families

Follow these steps to create and manage families in your church:

Step-by-Step Family Creation

  1. Navigate to Families: Click "Families" in the sidebar navigation
  2. Create New Family: Click the "Add New Family" button
  3. Enter Family Details:
    • Family Name: Enter the family surname or name
    • Family Head: Select the primary contact person
    • Address: Enter the family's address
    • Phone: Primary contact number
    • Email: Family email address
  4. Add Family Members: Select existing members to add to the family
  5. Set Relationships: Define how each member relates to the family head
  6. Save Family: Click "Save Family" to create the family unit
Pro Tips
  • Use consistent family naming
  • Designate a clear family head
  • Include all family members
  • Update family info regularly

Managing Family Members

Add, remove, and manage family members:

Adding Members to Families

  1. Go to the family details page
  2. Click "Add Family Member"
  3. Search for existing members
  4. Select the member to add
  5. Choose the relationship type
  6. Save the family member

Removing Family Members

  1. Navigate to family details
  2. Find the member to remove
  3. Click the "Remove" button
  4. Confirm the removal
  5. Member becomes unassigned

Best Practices

Family Management Best Practices

  • Keep family contact information updated

  • Outdated contact information
