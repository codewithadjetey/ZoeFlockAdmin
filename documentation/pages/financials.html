Tithe Management

Track member tithes with support for partial payments and multiple payment methods:

Recording Tithes

  1. Navigate to Tithes: Click "Tithes" in the sidebar
  2. Add New Tithe: Click "Add New Tithe" button
  3. Select Member: Choose the member from the dropdown
  4. Enter Amount: Input the tithe amount
  5. Choose Payment Method: Select from available options
  6. Add Reference: Optional reference number or notes
  7. Save Tithe: Click "Save Tithe" to record

Partial Payment System

Advanced Feature: Members can pay tithes in partial amounts over time.
  • Multiple Payments: Record multiple payments towards a single tithe
  • Balance Tracking: Automatic calculation of remaining balance
  • Payment History: Complete audit trail of all payments
  • Status Updates: Automatic status changes when fully paid
Payment Methods
  • Cash
  • Check
  • Bank Transfer
  • Mobile Money
  • Other

Donation Management

Track various types of donations and offerings:

Types of Donations

  • General Offerings: Regular Sunday offerings
  • Special Offerings: Designated for specific purposes
  • Building Fund: Contributions for construction projects
  • Missions: Support for missionary work
  • Benevolence: Help for members in need

Recording Donations

  1. Go to "Donations" section
  2. Click "Add New Donation"
  3. Select donation type
  4. Enter amount and details
  5. Choose payment method
  6. Save donation record
